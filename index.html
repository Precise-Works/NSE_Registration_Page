About Engineering Week 2026

Engineering Week 2026 organized by the Nigeria Society of Engineers (NSE), Uyo Branch, is a gathering designed to bring together engineers, students, innovators, researchers and industry professionals for a week of collaboration, learning and celebration.

This year's theme, “Smart Engineering Using Artificial Intelligence Tools”, focuses on how Artificial Intelligence is transforming engineering practice across multiple sectors.

The discussions and activities throughout the week will explore:

  • AI in construction and civil engineering
  • AI-powered manufacturing systems
  • Smart automation and robotics
  • Data-driven engineering decisions
  • Machine learning applications
  • Emerging engineering technologies
  • Ethics and future opportunities in AI engineering

Participants will have opportunities to learn from professionals, engage in networking sessions, attend exhibitions and experience hands-on innovation.

Engineering Week Schedule

Day 1 — Carnival Float & Novelty Football Match

Opening activities introducing Engineering Week with a public carnival, community engagement and football activities designed for interaction and awareness.

Day 2 — Thanksgiving Service

A thanksgiving and worship gathering for members and guests.

Day 3 — Media Day

Media appearances, interviews and public engagement to discuss the role of engineering and AI innovation.

Day 4 — Opening Ceremony / Conference / Exhibition

Official commencement of the conference featuring keynote sessions, professional discussions, exhibitions and presentations.

Day 5 — Indoor Games Grand Finale

Networking and recreational activities among attendees and participants.

Day 6 — Industrial / Technical Visit

Educational visits to industries and engineering facilities for practical exposure.

Day 7 — Commissioning of Engineering Complex

Closing event featuring commissioning activities and wrap-up of Engineering Week 2026.
